    My side projects

    I like to tinker. Here are some of my side projects from over the years.

    NōtoMe

    A simple iOS app that lets you quickly email a note to yourself

    The innovation? Swipe right to send the email to one address (e.g. personal), swipe left to send to another (e.g. work)

    App store link (still live!)

    PayMosaic

    A pay database formed by scraping the salary ranges from public job postings

    Built with Bubble and a few 3rd party services to help with scraping. Fun to see the law of large numbers emerge to result in data that was reasonably in-line with players like Pave, but which could give a much more real-time view of the market

    Fauxlinks

    A tool to power a company's internal "go-links" (a la Google)

    Built with a friend as a Bubble app + Chrome extension. Tested out the idea of replicating simple existing SaaS ideas but with a much lower cost basis by using no-code tools

    Slides Superuser Tools

    A Google Slides add-on that put a palette of various formatting and other poweruser utility features in a convenient sidebar

    The Google Slides equivalent of the internal Powerpoint plugins that many consulting firms have

    Prioritab

    A Google Chrome new tab page that let you track your top priorities for today, this week and this month

    My first ever published side project!
